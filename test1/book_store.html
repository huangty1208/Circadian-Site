Our Mission : It all starts with the owner, Sally

She wants her business to be more than a bookstore. She envisions this to be a place where there will be regular reading of poetry, literary discussions, book club meetings, and reading of children's stories on weekends. She herself is an avid reader and would like to attract a clientele that appreciates good books and discourse over coffee and snacks.
